# Vedabyte — Private Local AI for Your Household

AI without the anxiety. No data harvesting. No surprises. Your AI. Your House. Your Rules.

## What is Vedabyte?

<img width="1065" height="680" alt="Screenshot 2026-07-11 at 5 35 20 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1d3bbc54-1d68-4f8e-ab0c-34432be516ae" />

Vedabyte is a desktop application that turns your home computer into a private, locally-hosted AI platform for your entire household. Every conversation, every question, and every interaction stays entirely on your own device—nothing is ever uploaded to the cloud or sent to external servers. 

In a world where AI companies routinely profit from your private data, expose children to unmonitored content, and track your habits, Vedabyte gives you complete ownership back. It is AI built for real families who value true privacy, security, and peace of mind.

## Key Features

### 🏠 100% Private Local AI
* [cite_start]**Runs on Your Hardware:** Everything is processed right on your Mac[cite: 2827]. [cite_start]No internet-dependent cloud servers, no remote profiles, and zero tracking[cite: 2831].
* [cite_start]**Smart Local Memory:** Includes an intelligent memory system that learns your preferences over time, stored securely on your machine[cite: 2832].
* [cite_start]**Multi-User Profiles:** Set up separate profiles for everyone in your home without compromising their individual privacy[cite: 2846].

### 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family & Parental Controls
* **Message Monitoring:** Every conversation between a child profile and the AI can be monitored. [cite_start]If a digital boundary is crossed, parents are notified immediately[cite: 2833].
* **Household Usage Reports:** Ditch the blind spot. [cite_start]View simple, transparent daily breakdowns from your phone showing time active, general topics discussed, and flag counts[cite: 2833].
* [cite_start]**Screen Time & Limits:** Easily set allowed hours of the day or strict daily time limits per child profile to keep screen habits healthy[cite: 2833].
* **Memory Management:** View or safely clear any user profile's AI memory whenever you choose.

### 🎓 Interactive AI Tutor Workspace
* [cite_start]**Personalized Guided Learning:** A dedicated study space designed to assist with homework, practice questions, and subject comprehension[cite: 2834].
* [cite_start]**Adapts to Each Student:** The AI learns each child's strengths, weaknesses, and academic pace, tailoring its support to match their level over time[cite: 2834].
* [cite_start]**Private Progress:** Keep academic history, essays, and creative exercises local and secure[cite: 2835].
